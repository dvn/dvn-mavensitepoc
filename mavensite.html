"dvn" is the project.artifactId, for example.

These seem to be Maven "goals":

  • dvn:copy-dependencies copies the dependencies (from the compile scope) to the lib folder.
  • dvn:compile compiles your application.
  • dvn:test tests your application.
  • dvn:package packages your application as a Jar and a Zip.
  • dvn:clean cleans the project.
  • dvn:run runs the application (ctrl-c to exit).
